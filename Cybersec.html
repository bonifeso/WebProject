WELCOME TO CYBERSECURITY

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting internet-connected systems,
including hardware, software, and data, from cyberthreats.
These threats can come in a variety of forms,
including:

  • malware
  • ransomware
  • phishing attacks
  • denial-of-service (DoS) attacks

The goal of cybersecurity is to prevent these attacks from happening, or to minimize the damage they cause if they do happen.

An introduction to security awareness; why its important, the impact of being attacked, different threat actors and basic account security.

Understanding how common attacks occur will aid improve your cyber hygiene to stay safer online.
